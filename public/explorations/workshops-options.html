Five ways to make § 3 Workshops carry its own weight.

The current landing-page § 3 uses a 50/50 past · upcoming split. The past side is rich; the upcoming side is sparse — because workshop ii has no date, venue, or modules yet. The asymmetry reads as emptiness. Below are five directions for fixing it, rendered with the same brand tokens, fonts, and real assets as the live site.

Scroll through, jump between options with the tabs above, and use the comparison table at the bottom to weigh the trade-offs. The decision is yours; once you pick one (or a hybrid, or request a sixth), it gets ported into the real src/pages/index.astro.
