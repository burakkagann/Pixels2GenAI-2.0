Clean slate · prior five discarded

Three directions, each its own register.

The earlier five options were variations on one metaphor — rectangle next to rectangle, past card meets upcoming card. Same skeleton dressed differently. Below are three directions that throw that skeleton out and commit to a different visual idea each.

A treats the workshop as a moment photographed, with the poster as anchor and copy as caption. B treats the section as the cohort itself — nine fractals as anonymous participant portraits. C treats the workshop as a scientific specimen in an archival record, with workshop ii as Specimen 002 · in planning.

Same brand tokens · same real assets · no source files edited
Alt A · Moment Editorial spread · poster as anchor

The workshop is a moment, photographed.

Strip out every container. The poster takes the left half of the section like a magazine ad. Copy floats right in editorial whitespace. No card, no dl, no grid — just type and one image, trusting the brand to carry it. The workshop becomes a thing that happened, not a thing being explained.

Alt B · Roll call The section is the cohort

The cohort, not the workshop.
